Our Business


Welcome to Your Local Eco-Friendly Laundrette
Family-run since 1984 At the heart of the community for over four decades, our family-run laundrette blends trusted service with forward-thinking sustainability. We use the latest eco-conscious washing machines and dryers powered by solar energy, alongside cruelty-free, plant-based detergents for a fresh, guilt-free clean.

Step inside and you’ll find more than just washers and dryers—soft, comfortable furnishings and a welcoming coffee shop make it easy to relax while your laundry gets done.


Because doing the right thing for the planet should feel just as good as a fresh pile of warm clothes.

ENVIROMENT

Our Planet


Doing out bit for the planet
FreshCycle Laundrette Clean Clothes, Cleaner Future At FreshCycle, we're reimagining laundry with the planet in mind. Powered by solar panels and air-source heat pumps, our laundrette dramatically reduces carbon emissions while keeping your clothes perfectly fresh. We’ve built our space using recyclable materials wherever possible and operate on a model that champions resource efficiency and zero waste. We’ve engineered every detail for sustainability: Low-water-use machines that clean brilliantly without draining the planet Precision heating that only runs when needed, minimizing energy consumption Routine maintenance to ensure optimal machine performance and longevity Responsible waste disposal, with nothing going to landfill We're not just offering a service—we're inviting our community to be part of a movement. By choosing FreshCycle, you're supporting a circular, cleaner way to live. Because when it comes to laundry, less impact makes a bigger difference.


Working to make this planet a better place for future generations.
